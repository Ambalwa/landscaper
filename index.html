Welcome to Landscaper

Salis Landscaping is an award winning design and build landscape firm servicing the greater Eatern area. We truly believe we are enriching people’s lives through landscaping. Our goal is to create your oasis or dream landscape for you and your family to enjoy for years to come.

Whether that is creating a low maintenance yard or one where hours can be spent working in the garden – we design and build custom landscapes that fit your vision and lifestyle.
